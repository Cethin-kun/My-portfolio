🌊🌊🌊 ~ Ocean Waves ~ 🌊🌊🌊

Ever since I was a child I loved collecting toys, from LPs to My Little Pony. Now I love collecting things that peak my interest, like figurines from my favorite anime, merch of my favorite characters, and more. Currently my favorite character is Leviathan; I have the most merch of him right now, and more is on the way. I also collect pins and keychains of characters I like.

🌊🌊🌊 ~ Sea Treasures ~ 🌊🌊🌊

Like I said, I collect a lot of Leviathan merch but I also make my own. I create prints of him, stickers, and custom cards with effects like shiny, matte, or glittery finishes. Each piece is carefully crafted to feel special and unique.

🌊🌊🌊 ~ Display Your Ocean ~ 🌊🌊🌊

I love showing my devotion by making Ita bags. Ita bags are a great way to display pins, keychains, and plushies safely, with removable compartments to swap merch for different themes. It’s a creative way to show my favorite characters to others.
